	{\True $2a+b=0$}

	{$2a-b=0$}

		$$x=\dfrac{\pi}{2} \Rightarrow t= 2.$$
		$$I =\displaystyle \int \limits _{2}^{\tfrac{5}{2}} \dfrac{1}{t}\mathrm{\,d}t = \ln t \bigg | _{2}^{\tfrac{5}{2}} = \ln 5 - 2 \ln 2.$$
		Suy ra $a=1$, $b=-2$.\\
		Vậy $2a+b=0.$
	}
