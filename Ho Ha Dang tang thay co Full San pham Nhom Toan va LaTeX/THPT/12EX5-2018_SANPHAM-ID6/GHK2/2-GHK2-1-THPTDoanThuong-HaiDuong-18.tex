\sqrt{2t^2 -2t+5} +\sqrt{2t^2 -4t+4} &=& \sqrt{\left(\sqrt{2} t-\dfrac{\sqrt{2} }{2} \right)^2 +\left(\dfrac{3\sqrt{2} }{2} \right)^2 } +\sqrt{\left(\sqrt{2} -\sqrt{2} t\right)^2 +\left(\sqrt{2} \right)^2 }\\
&\ge& \sqrt{\left(\sqrt{2} -\dfrac{\sqrt{2} }{2} \right)^2 +\left(\dfrac{3\sqrt{2} }{2} +\sqrt{2} \right)^2 } =\sqrt{13}.
\end{eqnarray*}
(Áp dụng bất đẳng thức $\left|\overrightarrow{u}\right|+\left|\overrightarrow{v}\right|\ge \left|\vec{u}+\vec{v}\right|$; dấu bằng xảy ra khi hai véc tơ cùng hướng).\\

Như vậy $\sqrt{2t^2 -2t+5} +\sqrt{2t^2 -4t+4} \le \sqrt{13} \Leftrightarrow \sqrt{2t^2 -2t+5} +\sqrt{2t^2 -4t+4} =\sqrt{13} \Leftrightarrow t=\dfrac{4}{5} $.\\
Với $t=\dfrac{4}{5} \Rightarrow \log _{x} \dfrac{22}{3} =\dfrac{4}{5} \Leftrightarrow x=\sqrt[{4}]{\left(\dfrac{22}{3} \right)^5} \approx 12{,}1$.
	}
