		$\begin{aligned}
		\dfrac{2x-1}{x+5}=x-1&\Leftrightarrow 2x-1=(x-1)(x+5)\\
		&\Leftrightarrow 2x-1=x^2+4x-5\\
		&\Leftrightarrow x^2+2x-4=0\qquad(*)
		\end{aligned}$\\

	a=\dfrac{x_A+x_B}{2}=\dfrac{S}{2}=\dfrac{-2}{2}=-1\\
	b=a-1=-2
	\end{cases}$.\\
	Do đó: $T=2a^2+b=2(-1)^2-2=0$.}
\end{ex}
