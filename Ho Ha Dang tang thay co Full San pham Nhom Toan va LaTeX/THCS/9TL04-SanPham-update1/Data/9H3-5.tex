\begin{vd}

$$ \widehat{ASC}=\frac{1}{2}(\text{sđ}\wideparen{AB}-\text{sđ}\wideparen{MC}). \quad(1) $$
Ta có góc $\widehat{MCA}$ là góc nội tiếp trong $(O)$ chắn cung $AM$ nên
$$ \widehat{MCA}=\frac{1}{2}\text{sđ}\wideparen{AM}.\quad(2) $$
Theo giả thiết, ta có $AB=AC\Rightarrow \wideparen{AB}=\wideparen{AC}$. Thay vào $(1)$, ta có
\begin{eqnarray*}
	\widehat{ASC}&=&\frac{1}{2}(\text{sđ}\wideparen{AB}-\text{sđ}\wideparen{MC})\\
	&=&\frac{1}{2}\text{sđ}\wideparen{AM} . \quad(3)
\end{eqnarray*}
