{\True $7$}
{$2$}
{$3$}

}$.\\
Ta có:

$\Leftrightarrow \tan 4x-\tan 2x=4\tan 4x\tan 2x\tan x+4\tan x$
\\
$ \Leftrightarrow \tan 4x-\tan 2x=4\tan x\left(\tan 4x\tan 2x+1\right)$ \\ 
$ \Leftrightarrow \dfrac{\sin 4x}{\cos4x}-\dfrac{\sin 2x}{\cos2x}=4\dfrac{\sin x}{\cos x}\left(\dfrac{\sin 4x}{\cos4x}\cdot\dfrac{\sin 2x}{\cos2x}+1\right)$ \\ 
\\
$\Leftrightarrow \dfrac{\sin 4x\cos2x-\sin 2x\cos4x}{\cos4x\cos2x}=4\dfrac{\sin x}{\cos x}\cdot\dfrac{\sin 2x\sin 4x+\cos2x\cos4x}{\cos2x\cos4x}$\\
$\Leftrightarrow \sin 2x\cos x=4\sin x\cos2x$\\
$\Leftrightarrow 2\sin x\cos^2x=4\sin x\cos2x$\\
