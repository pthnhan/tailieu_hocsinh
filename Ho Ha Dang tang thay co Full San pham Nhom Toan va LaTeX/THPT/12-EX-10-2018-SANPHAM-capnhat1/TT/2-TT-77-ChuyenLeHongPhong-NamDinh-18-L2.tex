Suy ra $v_n=\dfrac{1}{u_1-2}-\dfrac{1}{u_2-2}+\dfrac{1}{u_2-2}-\dfrac{1}{u_3-2}+\cdots+\dfrac{1}{u_n-2}-\dfrac{1}{u_{n+1}-2}=\dfrac{1}{u_1-2}-\dfrac{1}{u_{n+1}-2}.$\\
Do đó $\displaystyle \lim_{x\to+\infty}v_n=\displaystyle \lim_{x\to+\infty}\left(\dfrac{1}{u_1-2}-\dfrac{1}{u_{n+1}-2}\right)=\dfrac{1}{u_1-2}=1.$
}
\end{ex}
