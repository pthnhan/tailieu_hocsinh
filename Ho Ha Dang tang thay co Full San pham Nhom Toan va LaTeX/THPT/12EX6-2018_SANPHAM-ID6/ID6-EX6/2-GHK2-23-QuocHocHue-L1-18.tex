{$\dfrac{a^3}{2}$}
{$\dfrac{a^3\sqrt{2}}{4}$}

\begin{align*}
\dfrac{SB'}{SB}=\dfrac{SB'\cdot SB}{SB^2}=\dfrac{SA^2}{SA^2+AB^2}=\dfrac{4}{5}.
\end{align*}

\end{align*}
