\begin{ex}%[Đề thi thử GHKII, Ngô Quyền, Quảng Ninh 2018]%[Hung Tran, 12EX-6,18]%[1D1K3-1]
Số nghiệm của phương trình $\sin^4x+\cos^4x=\dfrac{\sin^22x+1}{2}$ trong đoạn $\left[-\dfrac{\pi}{2};\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right]$ là
\choice
{$1$}
{$2$}
{\True $4$}
{$3$}
\loigiai{
Phương trình đã cho tương đương với
\begin{eqnarray*}
2\left( \sin^4x+\cos^4x\right)=\sin^22x+1 &\Leftrightarrow & 2\left(1-\dfrac{1}{2}\sin^22x\right)= \sin^22x+1\\
&\Leftrightarrow &\sin^22x=\dfrac{1}{2}\Leftrightarrow \hoac{&\sin 2x=\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\\&\sin 2x=-\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}.}
\end{eqnarray*}
\begin{enumerate}
\item[\textbf{TH1:}] $\sin 2x=\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\Leftrightarrow \hoac{&x=\dfrac{\pi}{8}+k\pi\\&x=\dfrac{3\pi}{8}+k\pi},\ k\in\mathbb{Z}$.\\
$\circ $ $-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\le\dfrac{\pi}{8}+k\pi\le\dfrac{\pi}{2}\Leftrightarrow -\dfrac{5}{8}\le k\le \dfrac{3}{8}\Rightarrow k=0$.\\
$\circ $ $-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\le\dfrac{3\pi}{8}+k\pi\le\dfrac{\pi}{2}\Leftrightarrow -\dfrac{7}{8}\le k\le \dfrac{1}{8}\Rightarrow k=0$.
\item[\textbf{TH2:}] $\sin 2x=-\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\Leftrightarrow \hoac{&x=-\dfrac{\pi}{8}+k\pi\\&x=\dfrac{5\pi}{8}+k\pi},\ k\in\mathbb{Z}$.\\
$\circ $ $-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\le -\dfrac{\pi}{8}+k\pi\le\dfrac{\pi}{2}\Leftrightarrow -\dfrac{3}{8}\le k\le \dfrac{5}{8}\Rightarrow k=0$.\\
$\circ $ $-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\le\dfrac{5\pi}{8}+k\pi\le\dfrac{\pi}{2}\Leftrightarrow -\dfrac{9}{8}\le k\le -\dfrac{1}{8}\Rightarrow k=-1$.
\end{enumerate}
Vậy phương trình đã cho có $4$ nghiệm trên đoạn $\left[-\dfrac{\pi}{2};\dfrac{\pi}{2}\right]$.
